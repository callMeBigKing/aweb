Hotel Offers

Participants of GDN 2018 can get discounts on their bookings in the following hotels using the code "GDN 2018".
Please book your accommodation directly on the hotel's website in order to benefit from the discount (links are given below). Bookings on third online travel portals will not accept the GDN code. The code will expire on 10 June.
