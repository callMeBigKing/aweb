How to get to China

Nanjing Lukou International Airport is one of the important transportation hubs in Nanjing. It is an important airport, linking this city with many cities home and abroad. It is easily connected to Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou and all the other domestic cities in China. Nanjing Lukou International Airport serves direct flight to and from many Asian as well as European destination.

How to get to Nanjing

Nanjing is a railway hub with two main train stations in the city: Nanjing Railway Station and Nanjing South Railway Station. Meanwhile, Nanjing Lukou International Airport is an important airport, linking this city with many cities home and abroad. Flights to domestic cities such as Beijing, Shanghai and Guangzhou can be found at your arrival at airport. From abovementioned information, when you arrive in China, you can arrive in Nanjing through following ways.

By Plane (Nanjing Lukou International Airport).

By high-speed rail(Nanjing South Railway Station).

By Train(Nanjing Railway Station).

How to get to conference location

When you arrive in Nanjing, you can reach the conference location (No. 1528 Shuanglong Avenue, Jiangning District, Nanjing) either by subway or by taxi.

1. By subway

  • (1) Nanjing South Railway Station — Subway line 1 — The ShengTai Road subway station — Nanjing Lakehome (No. 1528 Shuanglong Avenue, Jiangning District, Nanjing).
  • (2) Nanjing South Railway Station — Subway line 1—The ShengTai Road subway station— Nanjing Lakehome (No. 1528 Shuanglong Avenue, Jiangning District, Nanjing).
  • (3) Nanjing Lukou International Airport — Subway line S1 — Subway line 1—The ShengTai Road subway station— Nanjing Lakehome (No. 1528 Shuanglong Avenue, Jiangning District, Nanjing).

2. By taxi

  • (1) Nanjing south railway station to Nanjing Lakehome may cost about 18 Yuan and 8minutes.
  • (2) Nanjing railway station to Nanjing Lakehome may cost about 49 Yuan and 22minutes.
  • (3) Nanjing Lukou international airport to Nanjing Lakehome may cost about 93Yuan and 35minutes.

Tips

Currency

The official name for the currency of China is Renminbi (RMB). It is denominated into Yuan (¥). Foreign currency can be exchanged for RMB at the airport, banks and hotels. Major credit cards are honored at most hotels. Banks usually open at 9:00 in the morning and close at 17:00 in the afternoon all working days.

Climate and Clothing

Mid-June is both summer and the start of tourist season in Nanjing. The average temperature in Nanjing is about 18°C / 66.4°F - 31°C / 87.8°F. Generally, clothes such as long-sleeved blouses are enough for this time in Nanjing.

Electricity

Electricity is supplied at 220V, 50Hz AC throughout China.It's better to prepare the conversion switch.
